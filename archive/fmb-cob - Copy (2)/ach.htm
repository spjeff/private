Definitions of SEC Codes:

  • CCD= Cash Concentration Disbursement entries to a corporate account
  • PPD= Predetermined payment or deposits to a consumer (personal) account
  • CTX= Corporate Trade Exchange - transactions that include EDI information
  • WEB= Web initiated entry - Electronic authorization through the internet to create an ACH entry
  • TEL= Telephone initiated entry - Verbal authorization by telephone to issue an ACH entry such as check by phone
