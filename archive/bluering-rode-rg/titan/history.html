History

 [<< Return to menu]

  • Began in 2015 as a partnership between the NRO, NGA, & USAF
    • Branched off from Washington Innovation Center
  • Designed as a risk reduction effort in support of cloud migration
    • IC had begun migration to commercial “one size fits most” cloud
    • Our predecessors took advantage of available deprecated hardware, the site’s high-bandwidth, and existing relationships
    • Host legacy HAF, NGA, and NRO programs as well as support to the IC
  • Provides compute, low-latency, and cloud-enabling services between multiple infrastructures
    • Started by hosting SIRIS, Detections-to-the-Cloud, WICIDEM-DEDAS, SnowKid, Topic Builder, and WeatherNow
    • Initial Data Services included hosted and facilitated links to imagery, FMV streaming and playback, and near real time weather forecasting
    • Grew to Innovation Support and Application hosting/Data Services expansion

  • Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and Data as a Service (DaaS)
    • NTM, commercial imagery, IMINT, Aircraft Tracks, Airborne FMV, and more
  • Rapid on-boarding for AI/ML projects and software development
  • Government Owned / Contractor Operated (GOCO)
    • Cloud service for a fraction of the cost of commercial providers
    • Large volume data storage and compute
    • Remote access for DoD & IC customers
  • Common Goal for Hosted Applications is exploit data veracity for better analysis and maximize decision making
    • Ingest, digest, analyze 100+ disparate data sets
    • Enable “sense-making” of disparate data sets
    • Enable the creation of visualizations of raw data into analytic results for exploitation, discovery, and tactical/strategic monitoring
    • Space for design, development, deployment, & operation of AI/ML analytics
    • Designed for a cloud architecture and scalability
